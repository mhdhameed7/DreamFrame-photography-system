My Journey

Eleanor Voss

Photography found me long before I found it. Growing up in the sun-drenched hills of Tuscany, I was captivated by the way light transformed ordinary scenes into something transcendent.

After studying Fine Arts at the École des Beaux-Arts in Paris and apprenticing with legendary portrait photographer Henri Moreau, I returned home with a singular vision: to create imagery that endures.

Over 10 years and 800+ sessions later, Lumière Studio has grown into a boutique atelier dedicated to the art of authentic storytelling. Every image we produce is a collaboration between light, emotion, and the irreplaceable human spirit.

"I don't take photographs. I receive them — from the light, from you, from the moment itself."
